Our NEMT Services

We provide Non Emergency Medical Transportation for those needing assistance to their medical appointments. We provide both car and wheelchair van service for seniors, veterans, and adults needing a little assistance:

  • Routine doctors appointments
  • Dialysis appointments
  • Patient discharges: inpatient hospital stay, outpatient surgeries, and endoscopy procedures
  • Physical therapy
  • Workers’ compensation claims
  • Independent Medical Examiner (IME) 

We offer a range of assisted transportation services, which include:

  • Door-to-Door
  • Door-through-Door
  • Hand-to-Hand
  • Bedside-to-Bedside

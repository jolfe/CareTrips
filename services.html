Our NEMT Services

CareTrips provides wheelchair-accessible transportation for those requiring travel via wheelchair. All of our vehicles are clean, late-model minivans which allow passengers to sit comfortably in their wheelchair as they travel. All of our caring drivers are throughly vetted employees of CareTrips. We do not use a large network of drivers like Lyft or Uber!
